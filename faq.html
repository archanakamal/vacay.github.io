FAQs

1. What is included in the beach package?
Accommodation, breakfast, guided beach activities, and airport transfers. Premium packages include spa and private tours.
2. Can I reschedule my booking?
Yes, bookings can be rescheduled up to 72 hours before the visit with no additional charge.
3. Are group discounts available?
Groups of 6+ are eligible for discounts. Contact support for a custom quote.
4. What should I pack?
Pack light clothing, swimwear, sunscreen, sunglasses, sandals, and a reusable water bottle.
5. Is Wi-Fi available?
Yes, all resorts have complimentary Wi-Fi, though speed may vary in remote areas.
